Spring 2015 GEMS Workshop:
Geometry and Art

The Association for Women in Mathematics (AWM) is holding a third Girls Engaged in Math and Science (GEMS) Workshop for girls in 6th-9th grade. The AWM provides fun, free activities and mathematical prizes. [Flyer]

Each session is self-contained, and girls may register for as many sessions as they wish. This semester we will explore various areas of geometry! Please see below for descriptions.
